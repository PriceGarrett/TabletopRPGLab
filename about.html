A little about us...

Tabletop RPG Lab hopes to be the ultimate RPG sandbox available online. This is a project by BYU student Garrett Price. In the coming months, this site will become a hub to play randomly generated RPG dungeons, player-led campaigns, and upload and share your own custom characters, weapons, rulesets, and countless other features.

Plans

  • In-depth character customization
  • Randomly generated mini-dungeons
  • Player-led game sessions, with a game-master and up to 4 players
  • Custom campaigns, characters, weapons, enemies, NPC's uploaded by users
